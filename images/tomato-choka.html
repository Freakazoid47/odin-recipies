Tomato Choka

  • 6 tomatoes large
  • 4 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 scotch bonnet
  • 3 leaves of chadon benni
  • 2 pimento chopped
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbspn oil of choice or use water fry

Steps

  1. place tomaoes on open flame till skin is chared to your preference
  2. Chop onion,pimento,chadon benni and garlic into thin slices and set aside
  3. when tomatoes are finished place in bowl and crush into fine paste with fork or potato masher
  4. bring medium saucepan with canola oil or ovile oil to a high heat
  5. add garlic and fry till crisp and dark(if water frying add pinch of salt and pepper to make extra crispy)
  6. chop chadon benni fine and add squeeze of lime juice on cutting board and let rest for 5 mins
  7. add onions and chandon benni to tomatoes and mix till consistent texture is reached
  8. add scotch bonnet to open flame and roast till medium charred but still soft inside
  9. crush pepper into garlic pan and mix roughly
  10. add garlic into tomatoes mixture and serve with choice of Roti or pita

Enjoy!
