Sardine Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 Tins of sardine
  • 1 Large tomato
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Medium red onion
  • 3-4 Pimentos
  • 2 Tsp
  • 2 Tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp white pepper
  • 2 Leaves chadon benni

Steps

  1. Dice tomatoes and onions into half inch cubes
  2. Slice pimentos in half starting at stem and chop into thin slices
  3. Finely chop chadon benni and squeeze lime over it,let rest for 2-3 mins
  4. Open tins of sardine and drain all of liquid out into trash
  5. Place sardines in the bowl and mash with fork until consistent
  6. Add all ingredients into bowl and mix till uniform
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste
  8. Serves 2-3 (Pairs great with wheat crakers or toasted croissant with butter)

Enjoy!
